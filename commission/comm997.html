Date:         Tue, 9 Sep 1997 17:00:17 -0500
From: William Boltz 
Subject:      PNC Working Group Update

Joyce Appleby has asked me to let all of you know that a piece by group
member Tom Bender has been posted in a new section of our working group's
webpage, "Materials of Interest."  You may reach it by directing your
web-browser to the working group webpage.  The webpage address is:

        http://www.upenn.edu/pnc/commission

You will be prompted to enter the following information:

        username: commission
        password: interact

Find the "Community" listing in the index, and follow the link to Tom's
work from our group's webpage.  You'll also find there an archive of our
discussion to date.

This posting will be followed in the coming days by important articles by
Michael Walzer and Jean Elshtain.  These pieces are designed to serve as
background for the working group's deliberations and should be useful in
generating substantive discussion on the group's listserv.  If you would
like to have something posted, please contact me (boltz@pobox.upenn.edu)
or our Technology Coordinator, Mike Strong, (mstrong@pobox.upenn.edu). 

As Joyce has noted, this group has a marvelous opportunity to conduct
exciting work.  The task before us is large, but manageable.  Joyce will
be contacting you personally in the days ahead to discuss the work of our
group.  If I or any member of the Commission staff can be of any
assistance, as always, please contact us.
