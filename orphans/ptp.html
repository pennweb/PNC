The Penn Public Talk Project is here to help you! A new program of the Penn National Commission on Society, Culture and Community, the Public Talk Project is a broadly conceived national effort to improve the practices of robust discourse and civic engagement in ways that leads to stronger, more inclusive communities.

Use this site and learn how to lead the conversation in your community. Get direct, immediate help from one of dozens of leaders through our national network.

Grappling with a tough issue? Tell us and we'll link you to one of our partners in the American Public Talk Coalition. Our site lets you learn about everything from good discourse practice to issues that concern you.
